How does it work?

Zokoter is based on playing poker with multiple player at the same table. That gives you a huge statistical advantage. Players could be playing on real different computers, but in my case I am using one computer with multiple virtual machines. It uses enviorement of Coinpoker, crypto poker page, which can provide required anonymity for this program to work. Our players are later connected with sockets and they communicate and share cards. Based on this intel, they compute which cards cannot the opponent have, preflop rank or odds for winning.

Requirements

  • No player can have the same ip adress, so you're gonna be using vpn or proxies.
  • The size of the Coinpoker windows has to have a given size. This size can be achieved with Sizer.
  • At least 2 dollars for every player.
  • Unique Exodus wallet, phone number and email.
